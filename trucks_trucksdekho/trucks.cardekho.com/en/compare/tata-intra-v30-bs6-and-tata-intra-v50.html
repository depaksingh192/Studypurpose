              • Tata Intra V30

                • The Tata Intra V30 is a compact, four-tyre commercial vehicle designed for customers engaged in long-haul applications.
                • The Intra V30 is powered by a reliable 1.5-litre BS6-compliant diesel engine and boasts a best-in-class gradeability of 41 percent.
                • The Tata truck has multiple performance enablers such as an Electronic Power-Assisted Steering System (EPAS), a gear shift advisor and an eco switch for improving driver and vehicular performance.
                • Tata Motors offers a 2-year / 72,000 km warranty (whichever is earlier) for assurance.
                • For peace of mind, Tata Motors also has a 24-hour toll-free helpline phone number for vehicle-related assistance.

                Tata Intra V50

                • The Tata Intra V50 is robust since it comes with a tough chassis and load body design.
                • It comes with a long load body measuring 9.8 feet in length.
                • The Intra V50 boasts a best-in-class gradeability of 37 percent for tackling inclines.
                • The truck is equipped with a gear shift advisor feature and a fuel economy switch to enhance fuel efficiency.
                • Tata Motors offers 2 years/72,000km warranty (whichever is earlier) for assurance.
                • For peace of mind, Tata Motors also has a 24-hour toll-free helpline phone number for assistance.

              • Tata Intra V30

                • Tata Motors could have offered a fleet management solution for improving turnaround time, fleet efficiency and vehicular performance.
                • Adjustable headrests are not offered even as an optional feature.
                • The Tata Intra V30 does come with air conditioning as a standard option.

                Tata Intra V50

                • Tata Motors could have provided fleet management solutions/apps for the Intra V50 as a standard feature.
                • Adjustable headrests are not offered even as an optional feature.
                • The Tata Intra V50 does not come with air conditioning as a standard option.
